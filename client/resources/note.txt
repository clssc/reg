@@block{en}
In the next screen you will be asked to fill out our registration form,
one registration form per family. Before we get started, please read the
following registration notes:<br />
<ol>
  <li>All students must be 5 years or older on the first day of the school year.</li>
  <li>The tuition is $900 (or $800 for early registration). Officers, teachers,
  and coaches who meet tuition reduction criteria will be refunded after the
  school year begins.</li>
  <li>Registration deadline and early bird discount deadline are determined by
  payment postmark.</li>
  <li>Each family, except staff and some teachers, shall serve 20 hours
  per year. Each family is required to maintain the $200 service deposit at
  time of registration. For families departing the school, the refund for family service point
  deposit must be requested within one year from the last day of the academic
  year in which they depart. After that time, no refund will be granted.</li>
  <li>Students applying for school language credit must take one designated
  extra-curricular class and pay for the associated fees in addition to the
  regular 3 hours of Chinese instruction each week (total of 4 hours per week).
  The pertinent rules (including subsequent updates) for credit class which are
  published on the school website shall govern.</li>
  <li>Extra-curricular activities are held between 11:45 A.M.-12:45 P.M. The
  fees for these activities are not included in the tuition. The pertinent
  rules (including subsequent updates) for these activities which are published
  on the school website shall govern.</li>
  <li>Registration fee is $100 and non-refundable. Bounced check fee is
  $30/each. Detailed rules regarding <a href="http://westsidechineseschool.weebly.com/tuition-fees-enrollment--refund-2341636027-3538720874332873686436027.html" target="_blank">
  tuition fees and refund policy</a> can be found on the school web site.</li>
</ol>
@@endblock
