at least one parent/guardian. For adult students, please fill in yourself as the parent
so that you will receive e-mail communications from the school.<br />
<b>To go back to a previous screen to make corrections, please click on the
&quot;Previous&quot; button and not the browser&apos;s back button.  Clicking the browser&apos;s
back button will clear the form and you will have to re-enter your information. </b><br />

@@endblock

@@block{tc}
我們將從填寫家長/監護人的資料開始。每個家庭至少需有一位家長/監護人。參加成人班的學生請在家長欄填入您個人的資料,
以便學校能夠以電子郵件的形式與您聯絡。<br/>
<b>由於系統設計上的限制, 若您需要回到上一頁去更正資料時,
請按頁面上的「上一頁」而不是您的瀏覽器所提供的上一頁鈕。如果您按到了瀏覽器的上一頁鈕,
您將被迫重新輸入全部的資料。</b><br />

@@endblock
