Note for New Students
新生註冊說明
新生注册说明
In the next screen you will be asked to fill out our registration form, one registration form per family. Before we get started, please read the following registration notes:
  1. All students must be 5 years or older on the first day of the school year.
  2. The tuition is $700 (or $600 for early registration). Officers, teachers, and coaches who meet tuition reduction criteria will be refunded after the school year begins.
  3. Students whose completed registrations are postmarked by 7/31/2014 will receive the textbooks on the first day of school. Students who register after 7/31/2014 are not guaranteed to receive the textbooks on the first day of school.
  4. Registration deadline and early bird discount deadline are determined by payment postmark.
  5. Each family, except staff and some teachers, shall serve 20 hours per year. Each family is required to maintain the $200 service deposit at time of registration. For families departing the school, the refund for family service point deposit must be requested within one year from the last day of the academic year in which they depart. After that time, no refund will be granted.
  6. Students applying for school language credit must take one designated extra-curricular class in addition to the regular 3 hours of Chinese instruction each week (total of 4 hours per week). Those who miss more than 12 hours of lessons will not be granted credits. Credit students shall submit application form (available upon request in the office), decide extra-curricular class and pay for the associated fee on the first class of the school year.
  7. Extracurricular classes (EC) are optional and open to all students and parents. Fees of EC are not included in the tuition and will be collected when you register for EC at the beginning of the school year.
  8. Registration fee is $100 and non-refundable. Bounced check fee is $30/each.
